Our commitment to privacy

We take privacy extremely seriously

Your personal information is just that, it contains identifying information of who you are.
We run our core operations around this sentiment, anything we do; we always consider
how it can affect your privacy. We store your data on extremely secure, seperately hosted
cloud servers with DDoS Protection, Two Factor Authentication systems and specific SSH
and SFTP protocols that only our team have access to.

Threats get mitigated

We use high level DDoS Protection to prevent threats!

Over 6 Million network bots get stopped in their tracks thanks to our DDoS protection
providers (  Cloudflare and    Cosmic Guard). We stop many different attempts at
taking down our systems all the time meaning your services are going to be online
99.99% of the time!
