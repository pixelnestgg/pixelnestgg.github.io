What it's like to work at Pixelnest

Pixelnest.gg is an amazing place to work. It's a fast-paced, innovative company with a strong team culture. The people are friendly and supportive, and the work is always interesting. There are also plenty of opportunities for growth and development.

Our amazing benefits

Get awesome benefits when you join the team!

Monthly Pay

Receive your payments reliably and on time with Pixelnest! Our payment system ensures that you get paid on the first Thursday of every month without fail.

Staff Discounts

Unlock exclusive discounts and a generous spending allowance at our store.

Staff Discounts

Enjoy your time off without any worries about missed pay! At Pixelnest, we offer our employees 40 holiday days per year with paid time off.

Performance Reviews

We believe in recognizing your hard work. That's why we review your performance six times during your first year, and every three months thereafter. 

Service Rewards

We recognize and reward continuous service at every step of your employment journey with us.

Learning & Development

We firmly believe in providing equal opportunities to all at Pixelnest. If you have the drive to learn, grow, and advance your career, we will support you every step of the way.
