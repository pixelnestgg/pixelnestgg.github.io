What is Pixelnest?

Learn about Pixelnest

Pixelnest is a small cloud infrastructure company. Our core principles are Security, Speed, Reliability and Customer Satisfaction.

We have taken a low-profit, high engagement approach to how we sell our products. We offer our products at super low prices, we make around a 20-27% profit on our products, which in the hosting industry is super low compared to our competitors which take a staggering ~60% profit margin* . The reason we do this is because we are highly engaged with our customers, the moment a customer makes a purchase they are a part of the Pixelnest family, our team, including the executives, are seen communicating to our community very often through our Discord!

*  According to articles released Digital Ocean takes a net 60% profit each year.
