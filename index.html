Important Announcement - Closure of Pixelnest Services

Dear Valued Pixelnest Users,
We are announcing important news regarding the future of Pixelnest.

After much consideration and evaluation, we regret to inform you that Pixelnest will be ceasing its operations and closing its services as of September 10, 2023.

Reason for Closure: Financial Troubles

The decision to close Pixelnest has been a difficult one, and it is primarily driven by ongoing financial challenges that have affected our ability to sustain the company's operations. We understand the impact this may have on our loyal users, and we want to assure you that this decision was not made lightly.

Smooth Transition and Support

As we move towards the closure date, our main priority is to ensure a smooth transition for all our users. We encourage you to make necessary arrangements to migrate your data and services to alternative providers. If you require any assistance or have questions during this transition period, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at https://pxln.cc/contact. We are here to provide the support you need.
