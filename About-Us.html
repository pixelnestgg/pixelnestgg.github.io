The start of Pixelnest

How Pixelnest came to be

Pixelnest started out offering Discord bot hosting and Minecraft server hosting on the 29th of April 2022.
It was just 3 of us at the time with big plans on creating the next amazing hosting company and as far as
that we think we have done exactly that. We continued to launch new products, gain new partners, access
new communities and improve our systems which is where we get to now with a team of 6 and an ever
growing company.

Looking to the future

What we look to be possible with Pixelnest

We are looking to push Pixelnest, we have already revolutionized how Discord partnerships work and
we look to do so much more, not just with our community but expanding the boundaries in what is
possible in cloud computing. We look to make it both extremely affordable, simple and fast for every
single user out there.

Here are just a few things we have planned for in the coming years

Localized Pricing

We think that everyone should have a shot
at online development and we plan to
make that easier with localized pricing.

Fully Hands On

We want to deploy our own servers, in our
own space where we can have hands on
maintenance and security with them

Hardware Recycling

We plan to use our hardware for aslong as
we can with it still being extremely high
performance.

Grow our team

We are going to continue to grow our team
of passionate developers to keep bringing
our customers amazing new products.
