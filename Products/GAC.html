Frequently Asked Questions

We got the answers to your questions!

Can I host a Discord bot / Minecraft server on a GAC?

Technically yes but we don't recommend it. It will be alot harder to configure your Discord bot / Minecraft server using a GAC as our panel is setup not to support certain features as the GAC is only mean't for hosting Generic web apps.

Do I get my own IPV4 address?

Not as of now. With the GAC line we only offer a ported IP which you can then setup to be accessed through a DNS provider.

How fast will I receive my order?

Your order will be processed as soon as we get it in most cases.

For larger orders we have to verify that your account information is correct to prevent against fraud.
