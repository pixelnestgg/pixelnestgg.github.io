Partnership Policy

Last Updated: 06/07/2023
Effective as of: 24/08/2023

Introduction

Your role as a partner

As a partner at Pixelnest you have a higher more appreciated role within the community. As a partner you are expected to use this role correctly and not abuse it. You may be given special priveliges within the community which must be respected. As a partner you will represent Pixelnest and what is has to offer and when doing so we expect the best of the best. We look for partners who are thrilled to provide a positive atmosphere not only when interacting with the Pixelnest community but also when interacting with your own server members.

Failure to uphold standards

Further into this policy you will be shown the standards we expect from our partners, these standards are to be upheld by you and your team. Failure to uphold standards and keep your community safe and clean can lead to a warning or removal from the partnership program. Not only do we expect you to uphold the Partnership Policy to high standards but also the Terms of Service and the Acceptable Use Policy to ensure when you are using the Pixelnest platform you are doing so in a safe and respectful way.

Partnership Quality Assurance

Third parties

Pixelnest recognizes that partnerships may involve interactions with third-party entities, such as vendors, suppliers, or subcontractors. While these third parties may play a role in supporting our partners' operations, it is essential to uphold our quality assurance standards throughout the partnership ecosystem. This section outlines the guidelines and expectations related to third-party involvement in the Pixelnest Partner Program.

Selection of Third Parties:
- When engaging third parties, partners are responsible for selecting reputable and reliable entities that align with Pixelnest's values and standards.
- Partners must conduct due diligence to ensure that third parties possess the necessary qualifications, expertise, and resources to deliver quality services or products.

Compliance with Third-Party Terms of Service:
- Partners must strictly adhere to the terms of service specified by the third-party services or platforms they engage with.
- Compliance includes following any usage restrictions, licensing agreements, privacy policies, and intellectual property guidelines set forth by these third parties.

Mature & Adult Content

As a participant in the Pixelnest Partner Program, it is important to maintain a partnership ecosystem that aligns with our values and caters to a wide range of audiences. This section outlines the policy regarding the prohibition of partners primarily focused on mature or adult content.

Content Alignment with Community Standards:
- Partners are expected to maintain content that is suitable for a general audience and upholds community standards.
- Content should be free from explicit, offensive, or sexually explicit material that may be considered mature or adult-oriented.

Primary Focus Restrictions:
- Partners are not permitted to primarily focus on producing, promoting, or distributing mature or adult content.
- The primary purpose of the partnership should be centered around other areas such as technology, gaming, creativity, or educational content.

Compliance with Legal and Platform Guidelines:
- Partners must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and guidelines related to content restrictions, including those set by platforms or services they utilize.
- This includes adhering to the terms of service and community guidelines of third-party platforms involved in content distribution.

Review and Monitoring:
- Pixelnest reserves the right to review partner content periodically to ensure compliance with content restrictions.
- Regular monitoring may be conducted to ensure partners are not primarily focused on mature or adult content.

Consequences of Non-Compliance:
- Failure to comply with the content restrictions policy may result in a warning, suspension, or termination of the partnership agreement.
- Pixelnest maintains a commitment to providing a safe and inclusive environment for our community, and strict enforcement of this policy is essential to uphold these standards.

Organization and Professionalism

Community Management:
- Partners are responsible for creating and maintaining a well-organized and engaging community environment.
- Community guidelines should be established and clearly communicated to promote positive interactions, respect, and inclusivity among community members.

Social Media Presence:
- Partners should maintain an active and professional social media presence aligned with the values and image of Pixelnest.
- Content shared on social channels should be relevant, informative, and appealing to the target audience.

Communication and Responsiveness:
- Partners should strive to maintain regular communication and engagement with their community and social media followers.
- Promptly responding to inquiries, feedback, and concerns helps foster trust, loyalty, and a sense of community.

Professional Conduct:
- Partners are expected to uphold professional conduct when interacting with community members and on social media platforms.
- Avoiding offensive language, personal attacks, or any behavior that may tarnish the reputation of Pixelnest is crucial.

Brand Consistency:
- Partners should ensure brand consistency in their community and social media channels by utilizing approved Pixelnest logos, graphics, and branding elements.
- Branding guidelines provided by Pixelnest should be followed to maintain a cohesive and recognizable presence.

Compliance with Platform Policies:
- Partners must adhere to the terms of service, community guidelines, and policies of the platforms they utilize for community and social media interactions.
- Compliance with these platform guidelines helps maintain a positive and respectful online environment.

Failure to comply may result in a warning, suspension or permenant termination of being apart of the Pixelnest partner program.

Local Laws

Partners are expected to follow all local laws of the country the partner resides in. Pixelnest takes no responsibility in upholding the law however if a partner is found to be breaking the law they will be terminated from the Pixelnest Partner Program.

Contractual Agreement

Upon being accepted as a partner each partnership representative is expected to sign a contractual agreement with Pixelnest both for their and our legal safety.

Hate speech

Every Pixelnest partner is regularly checked and is monitored for hate speech, racism, sexism, homophobia etc. to ensure that any negative or improperly moderated partner is terminated. Partners must moderate their communities to a high level to ensure that this never happens.
